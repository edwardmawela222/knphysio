Meet Kabelo Nyalungu

Kabelo Nyalungu is a dedicated and impact-driven physiotherapist with a strong focus on the management of musculoskeletal conditions, particularly back and neck pain.

His clinical approach is centred on accurate assessment, evidence-based treatment, and long-term rehabilitation to restore function and improve quality of life.

In addition to musculoskeletal care, Kabelo has a strong interest in sports physiotherapy, injury prevention, performance enhancement, and neurological rehabilitation.
